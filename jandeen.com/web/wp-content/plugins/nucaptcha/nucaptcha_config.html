NuCaptcha Configuration

NuCaptcha is a powerful next-generation Animated Captcha available for WordPress. It is both easier to use and more secure than common Captcha systems.

Find out more about the NuCaptcha WordPress Plugin.

Remember, you can sign in to your account through the NuCaptcha Console to configure additional features. Some configuration changes through the NuCaptcha Console, such as Themes, can take up to 30 minutes before they show up on your site.

Need assistance? Contact NuCaptcha Support any time - we're here to help!
