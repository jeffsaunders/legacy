Welcome to Dennis Tobler's...
Football Forecast Weekly

    The 2005 streaming video version of Football Forecast Weekly will air on this website and many more and therefore can now be seen worldwide with the click of a mouse. The National Show will be featured and archived on the GamingBroadcast.com website each week. This will be the thirteenth year of production for the popular football prediction show, based in the sports gaming "Capital of the World," Las Vegas.

This year's show will feature such illuminaries as Buzz Daly, who brings his vast knowledge and objective views of the industry. Buzz currently hosts his own radio show. His insights are priceless.

James "Jimbo" Burford will add his talents to the show by releasing each week, "Jimbo's Jumbo Parlay." Jimbo has terrorized Las Vegas Sportsbooks over the years with his 10-15 team parlays that have the Las Vegas 'Books quaking in their shoes!

Each week, special guests and the Football Forecast girls round out what the Orange County Register called "America's No.1 Football TV Show."

During the 2004 football season Mr. Tobler travelled worldwide consulting gaming operations and filming television programs involving casinos and sportsbooks. In 2005, the public is anxiously awaiting the revival of Football Forecast Weekly, don't miss it!
