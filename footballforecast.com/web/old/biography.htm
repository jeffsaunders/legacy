Since 1988, Dennis Tobler has been writing, producing, directing, starring in and distributing the popular nationally syndicated television show Football Forecast Weekly. The Football Forecast Weekly website has been the home page for professional Las Vegas handicapper extrordinaire Dennis Tobler, since 1996. Mr. Tobler has been a fixture in the sports gaming and horse race handicapping business in Las Vegas for over 20 years. As owner of the TSI Network, Tobler is one of the most sought after celebrities in the business. He has appeared as the principle talent on over 220 nationally televised shows and thousands of radio programs in the past 20 years. His expertise is unmatched and for that reason he acts as a consultant to many sports books around the world.


The 2004 season will find Mr. Tobler starring in the national television production Football Forecast Weekly, which is in its 13th year of production. Not only one of the longest running programs of it's type, it was tabbed by the Orange County Register in Anaheim, CA as the BEST SHOW EVER when it comes to imparting football information.

Dennis Tobler is a life-long sports participant and sports and horse race bettor. For the last 20+ years, the Nebraska-born and football-bred Tobler has been one of the country's top handicappers. He is one of a handful of experts who helps set the line in Las Vegas and offshore. His sources run very deep in this close knit business.

During the 1960's, Tobler was a two-time all-state football player in Nebraska, where they really know their football! During the 1970's, Tobler broke nearly every private bookmaker in the Midwest. By 1980, Tobler moved his tack to Las Vegas, where the rest is history.

Tobler has been a sports handicapper and entrepreneur since 1972. Doing business as TSI Network, he founded his sports information service in Las Vegas in 1982, then one of only four licensed services, to sell sports and horse racing handicapping information through "800," "900," and "976" telephone services.

Beginning in 1988, Tobler began television syndication of "Football Forecast Weekly" and horse racing specials. These infomercials advertise TSI's sports information service. With a potential viewing audience of 50 million households, these programs have consistently generated customers for TSI's handicapping service and worldwide industry products.

Since the inception of the offshore gaming industry, Mr. Tobler has become a major player in all aspects, from consulting on software and business setup to actually filming television programs in Antigua, St. Kitts, Curacao, and of course, Costa Rica.
