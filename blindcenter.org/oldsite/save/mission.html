The purpose of ISIGHT Inc. is to assist blind and visually impaired persons of all ages in reaching their highest physical, social, intellectual, and economic potential.

The challenges associated with blindness may be overcome with determination, education, training, and opportunity. Programs and activities offered by the Center are to assist blind and visually impaired persons. To achieve these objectives, there are three focus areas:

•Personal Development. Assisted living, in-home training, medical care, physical fitness and emotional support.
•Social Interaction. Arts and crafts, recreational outings, transportation assistance, lunches and entertainment.
•Meaningful employment. Job training, career development, vocational rehabilitation and placement.

This corporation shall engage in activities and exercise powers that further these charitable objectives.
