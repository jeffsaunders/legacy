What are Legal Nurse Consultants?

Legal Nurse Consultants (LNCs) are registered nurses that serve as a bridge between the medical and legal professions. Using their broad medical knowledge, LNCs provide attorneys with cutting edge information to win their cases. LNCs assist with the following types of cases:

-Medical Malpractice
-Personal Injury
-Product Liability
-Toxic Torts and Environmental Cases
-Workers' Compensation
-Criminal Law


What services do Tobler & Associates provide?

Legal Nurse Consultants practice in both plaintiff and defense
capacities in a variety of settings:

-Screen medical malpractice and personal injury cases for merit
-Analyze, organize and index medical records
-Discover missing pages and documents from medical records
-Investigate medical records for alterations and/or tampering
-Define diagnostic procedures and medical terms
-Identify adherence and/or deviations from the Standard of Care
-Assist with deposition
-Provide deposition summaries prior to trial
-Aid with and/or develop exhibit preparation
-Expert witness location
-Life care plan development
